Privacy Policy ​

Last updated: December 2025

Introduction ​

TechLaboratory ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information.

Information We Collect ​

Usage Information ​

We may collect information about how you interact with our website and open-source projects, including:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on pages
  • Links clicked
  • Browser type and version

GitHub and NPM ​

When you interact with our projects on GitHub or NPM, those platforms may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

How We Use Information ​

We use the information we collect to:

  • Improve our projects and documentation
  • Understand how our projects are being used
  • Respond to user inquiries and support requests

Third-Party Services ​

Our website and projects may use third-party services such as:

  • GitHub - for code hosting and collaboration
  • NPM - for package distribution
  • PayPal - for donations and support

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

Cookies ​

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Data Security ​

We implement reasonable security measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Open Source ​

All our projects are open source. Any code contributions you make to our projects will be publicly visible on GitHub.

Changes to This Policy ​

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us ​

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through:

  • GitHub: https://github.com/techlab
  • Email: Contact via our GitHub profile

Your Rights ​

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access your data
  • The right to correct inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to object to processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us through the channels listed above.
