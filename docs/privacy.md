# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: December 2025**

We may collect information about how you interact with our website and open-source projects, including:

- Time spent on pages
- Links clicked

When you interact with our projects on GitHub or NPM, those platforms may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

We use the information we collect to:

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.
